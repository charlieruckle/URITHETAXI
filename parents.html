Safety, Values & Your Questions

We believe in transparency. Here is how we support the well-being and success of every member.

What is your policy on hazing?

Theta Xi maintains a strict no-hazing policy aligned with URI and national fraternity standards.

How is academic success supported?

Members follow study expectations, mentorship, and accountability to help meet GPA goals.

Is the chapter house safe?

We follow campus and local safety standards, with regular checks and responsible house leadership.

How are finances handled?

Chapter finances are managed with transparency, clear budgeting, and ongoing oversight.
