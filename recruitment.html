COMMON QUESTIONS

Recruitment FAQ

What is recruitment?

Recruitment is a series of events where you can meet the brothers and learn if Theta Xi is the right fit.

Is there a GPA requirement?

We value academics and expect members to prioritize school. Details are shared during recruitment.

Does attending mean I have to join?

No. Recruitment is about exploring options and getting to know the chapter.
